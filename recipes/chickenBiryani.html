Chicken Biryani

Description

A classic Indian dish that combines aromatic basmati rice with tender chicken pieces, fragrant spices, and caramelized onions. This layered rice dish is a perfect blend of flavors and textures that will transport you straight to the streets of India.

Ingredients

  • Rice: 2 cups of premium basmati rice
  • Chicken: 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • Yogurt: 1 cup for marination
  • Onions: 2 large, thinly sliced
  • Spices: Biryani masala, saffron, cardamom, cinnamon, bay leaves
  • Herbs: Mint and coriander leaves
  • Other: Ghee, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies

Steps

  • Marinate chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt and spices, let it rest for 1 hour
  • Prepare rice: Cook basmati rice with whole spices until 70% done
  • Cook chicken: Cook marinated chicken until tender
  • Layer: Alternate layers of rice and chicken mixture
  • Dum cooking: Seal the pot and cook on low heat for 20 minutes
