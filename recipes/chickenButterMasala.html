Chicken Butter Masala

Description

A rich and creamy North Indian curry made with tender chicken pieces in a luscious tomato-based gravy. This popular dish is known for its slightly sweet taste and silky smooth texture, perfect with naan bread or rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken: 500g boneless chicken pieces
  • Butter: 2 tablespoons
  • Tomatoes: 4-5 medium sized, pureed
  • Cream: 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Spices: Garam masala, turmeric, red chili powder
  • Aromatics: Onions, ginger-garlic paste
  • Kasuri methi: Dried fenugreek leaves

Steps

  • Marinate chicken: Season chicken with yogurt and spices
  • Prepare gravy: Cook onions, tomatoes, and spices until smooth
  • Cook chicken: Add marinated chicken to the gravy
  • Finish: Add cream and butter, simmer until rich and creamy
  • Garnish: Top with cream and kasuri methi
